USMC Gear Borrowing Information:

USMC allows for it's members to borrow certain pieces of gear. However there are some conditions that need to be met before you can be allegeable to borrow gear. You will be required to sign a liability form and demonstrate a specified level of skill which varies between each piece of gear.
