USMC Gear Borrowing Information:

USMC allows for it's members to borrow certain pieces of gear. You will be required to sign a liability form and demonstrate a specified level of skill which varies between each piece of gear.

Gear Borrowing Form

This is the form you will need to complete. After completing the form you can either email it to example@surrey.ac.uk or print out and physically give the form to "whoever we decide will manage it".

Gear you can Borrow:
Boulder matts

To borrow boulder equipment you will need to demonstrate that you are able to spot a climber safely and that you have knowlage on where the best place to put boulder matts is on a boulder route. The following links will explain these 2 concepts in more detail.

Bouldering skills: how to spot when bouldering

USMC also follows a no trace left mentallity when using any public areas which most outdoor boulder problems will be located in, as such we expect all members that will be using club gear to treat the areas where you boulder with high amounts of respect.
